Key Course Insights
Healing overtakes Manifestation. Without top leaders' influence, Healing & Soul Work (7,863) is now nearly 2× Manifestation & Abundance (4,059). The community's genuine #1 need is to heal — karmic patterns, gut energy, emotional wounds, karma, and cord cutting.
Hanuman Sadhana is the true #1 course. With 1,126 enrollments it leads without any top-leader distortion. Devotional, culturally rooted content has an authentic paying audience that doesn't need celebrity practitioners to drive it.
Voice & Sound Healing is a true niche demand. 546 users whose dominant theme is voice/sound healing, avg 1.0 courses — single-purchase specialists. This audience has very specific needs and isn't being cross-sold effectively.
Meditation & Breathwork holds its own. 1,635 users — driven by Breathe, Tap (EFT), and Release programs. This segment persists even when free daily content (SoulSync) is removed, confirming paid demand for somatic tools.
Key 1-on-1 Insights
1-on-1 demand is leader-independent. The top 20 sessions are nearly identical to the full paid dataset — Tarot (374), Psychic Tarot (248), Akashic (180). Removing top leaders had zero impact on session bookings, confirming 1-on-1 is driven by user intent, not creator pull.
Healing theme rises in 1-on-1. Healing & Soul Work (753 bookings) now rivals Psychic & Divination more closely in proportional terms — with top-leader manifestation noise removed, Akashic Records, Soul Purpose, and Karmic Healing emerge as the #2 cluster.
1,329 pure 1-on-1 users — up from 1,207. More users in this dataset came in purely for a 1-on-1 session with no course purchase. This confirms a direct-to-session journey that top leaders were partially masking.
Overlap rate drops to 30.8%. Fewer cross-channel users without top leaders (vs 37.1% in full paid). Top leaders were converting course buyers into 1-on-1 bookers at a higher rate — a retention mechanic worth studying.
