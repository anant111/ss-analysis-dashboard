Top theme combinations booked in the same 1-on-1 journey:

81.9% of repeat 1-on-1 bookers cross themes — identical to full paid data. The 1-on-1 ecosystem is entirely organic and unaffected by leader course removal. Users start with Psychic/Tarot and evolve into Healing or Astrology — a deepening journey, not repetition.

Course Repeat Buyer Insights
Repeat rate drops from 47.2% to 36.2% without top leaders. The top-leader courses (Manifest Like A Millionaire, Energy Numbers) drove a significant chunk of multi-course buying through their bundled effect. The organic base still shows healthy repeat behavior — 36.2% is a strong rate for a spirituality platform.
Healing leads at every depth tier — no exceptions. From 2 courses all the way to 5+, Healing is the #1 theme. This is a complete reversal from full paid data where Manifestation led at 2–4 courses. The organic buyer starts with and deepens into healing work.
Karma + Cords is the new top pair (460×). Without the mega-manifestation bundle, the #1 co-purchase is an organic karmic clearing double-pack. The top 8 pairs are all healing, devotional, or somatic — a clean picture of what users truly come back for.
Breathe + Tap + Release is a 3-way cluster (254× each pair). These three somatic/breathwork courses form a functional toolkit that users buy as a unit. Formal bundling could reduce friction and grow this cluster further.
Voice Activation (445) and Voice-based Tools (226) are power-buyer staples. The Voice/Sound theme is strongly over-represented among multi-course buyers vs. single-course buyers — suggesting it's a discovery category that hooks users into deeper content exploration.
Session Repeat Buyer Insights
1-on-1 behavior is virtually identical to full paid data. Session counts, funnel shape, top sessions, and cross-theme behavior are unchanged — confirming that top-leader removal only affects the course ecosystem, not the practitioner booking side.
Healing overtakes Psychic at the Astrology crossover point. In this dataset, Astrology → Healing (37 combos) closes the gap with Astrology → Psychic (48), more so than in full paid. The Healing + Psychic combo (102) remains the top pair.
7.7% of multi-course buyers also book 1-on-1 (up from 5.4%). Among 5,029 multi-course buyers, 385 also book a 1-on-1 session. The healing-focused cohort is more likely to bridge to personal sessions — the upsell path is more naturally accessible here.
Birth Chart Analysis remains a repeat-booking magnet. 70 bookings among 309 multi-session users — the annual transit reading pattern holds strong. Natural subscription opportunity.
Hypnotherapy + Psychic combo (18) appears prominently. More visible in this dataset than full paid — users seeking mind reprogramming (hypnotherapy) pair it with psychic readings. An emerging cross-service journey worth watching.
