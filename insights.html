Key Course Insights
Manifestation is the #1 paid theme. Unlike free/all-user data where meditation dominated, paid users lead with Manifestation & Abundance (12,213 enrollments). "Manifest Like A Millionaire" and "Energy Numbers & Visualisation" both hit 2,786 — the top two spots tied.
Healing is close behind. Healing & Soul Work (11,381) is nearly equal to Manifestation — karmic relationships, core wounds, ancestral healing, cords & cleansing are all top paid items. Users are paying to process and release.
Spirituality & community converts well. Hanuman Sadhana (1,126), Pyaar (386), Parivar (386) — culturally rooted Indian spiritual content has a strong paid buyer base.
Voice & Sound Healing is a paid-only signal. With 1,084 enrollments, this theme barely appears in free content — it's a differentiated paid niche with clear demand.
Key 1-on-1 Insights
Psychic & Divination dominates at 57%. Tarot Card Reading (374), Psychic Tarot (248) and Akashic Records (180+38) make up the bulk. Paid users treat 1-on-1 as a personal reading service, not coaching.
Soul direction services convert at premium. Soul Purpose Plan (170) and Birth Chart Analysis (167) are high-value personal consultations — users paying for clarity on their life path.
1,207 pure 1-on-1 paid users — a distinct segment. These users skipped all courses and went straight to a personal session. They likely came in via practitioner referrals or specific service discovery — worth a targeted retention flow.
Animal Telepathy punches above its weight. 113 bookings with zero significant course demand — this niche has a loyal, ready-to-pay audience not being served in the course catalogue.
