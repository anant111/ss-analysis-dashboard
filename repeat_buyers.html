Top theme combinations booked in the same 1-on-1 journey:

81.9% of repeat 1-on-1 bookers cross themes. They don't keep returning for the same type of reading — they start with Psychic/Tarot and evolve into Healing or Astrology. This signals a deepening journey, not just repeat satisfaction with one service.
Psychic → Healing is the dominant cross-theme journey (121 users). Users who start with tarot readings eventually book Akashic Records, Soul Purpose, or Karmic Healing sessions. The reading surfaces awareness; the healing session addresses it.
Astrology pairs with both Psychic (51) and Healing (48). Astrology acts as a bridge service — users seek their chart reading, then move either toward readings (psychic) or toward action (healing). It's a high-conversion pivot point.
Course Repeat Buyer Insights
Nearly half of paid users buy more than 1 course. 47.2% (9,663) are multi-course buyers — this is an extremely healthy repeat rate. The platform's content breadth is driving sequential purchases, not just one-off attendance.
Manifestation leads at 2–4 courses; Healing takes over at 5+. Early buyers are drawn by outcome-driven manifestation content. As they deepen, they shift to healing and soul work. This is a clear content journey arc — each stage primes the next.
Strongest co-purchase pair: Energy Numbers + Manifest Like A Millionaire (2,786×). These two courses are bought together by every single buyer of either — they function as a bundle in practice. Formal bundling could reduce friction and increase this further.
Devotional pairs are highly sticky. Pyaar + Parivar (396×), Chant Gayatri + Prosperity (390×), Paisa + Pyaar (339×) — culturally rooted course pairs have near-perfect co-purchase rates, suggesting a tight community around this content vertical.
Multi-course users rarely upgrade to 1-on-1 (5.4%). Only 521 of 9,663 multi-course buyers also book a 1-on-1 session. There's a significant untapped upsell opportunity — the intent is there (they're engaged), but the bridge to personal sessions is missing.
Session Repeat Buyer Insights
Most 1-on-1 bookers stop at 1 session (83.9%). Only 309 of 1,920 book a second session. The main drop-off is after session 1 — better post-session follow-up flows could meaningfully increase this number.
Multi-session users split Psychic and Healing equally. Among users booking 3+ sessions, Healing & Soul Work (281 bookings) nearly matches Psychic & Divination (316). This is a key shift from single-session users where Psychic dominates at 57% — deeper engagement moves users toward therapeutic healing work.
62.8% of repeat session bookers also buy courses. This is the highest cross-channel overlap on the platform. Repeat 1-on-1 buyers are the most engaged, highest-LTV users — they should be prioritised in retention and course recommendation flows.
Birth Chart Analysis is a repeat-booking magnet. 70 bookings among just 309 multi-session users — buyers return annually for transit readings and life stage updates. This is the most natural subscription service on the platform.
1 user booked 12 sessions; another 11. The top 5 multi-session users each averaged 9+ sessions. These are de facto ongoing clients with specific practitioners — a membership or retainer model would formalise and protect this revenue.
